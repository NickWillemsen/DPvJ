About Us

Radioactive eSport (abbreviated Active) is an esport organisation based in the Netherlands. It was founded in October 2018, by Teotix "Finn Steenhagen".

Radioactive currently fields players in Fortnite. Radioactive currently has 4 teams playing Fortnite and a team full of designers. The organisation previously had a Call of Duty team based in England and also briefly owner an Fifa team.

What We Do

We are competing in Fortnite with our own eSports team named: "Radius". We also manage our own tournaments in Fortnite. Another thing we do is making montages on YouTube. For more information about us follow our social page: radioactive.gg.
